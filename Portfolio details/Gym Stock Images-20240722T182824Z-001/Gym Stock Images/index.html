Special Diet

boiled chicken

Boiled chicken is a powerhouse of lean protein that supports muscle growth and recovery. Its low-fat content makes it ideal for building lean muscle without extra calories. By incorporating boiled chicken into your diet, you’re fueling your body with essential amino acids that repair and build muscle after intense workouts

Bread salad

Bread salad is a great way to combine complex carbohydrates with fresh vegetables. The carbohydrates provide sustained energy for your workouts, while the veggies offer essential vitamins and minerals. This nutritious mix helps replenish your energy stores and supports muscle function and recovery.

Dry Fruits

Dry fruits are a concentrated source of nutrients and natural energy. Packed with vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats, they offer a quick energy boost and support muscle repair and growth. Including dry fruits in your diet provides the essential nutrients your body needs to perform at its best and recover effectively

Healthy Fruits

Healthy fruits are rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and natural sugars that fuel your workouts and aid in muscle recovery. Their high water content helps keep you hydrated, while their natural sugars provide a quick energy source. Including a variety of fruits in your diet supports overall health and enhances your gym performance.

Fresh Juice's

Fresh juices are an excellent way to hydrate and replenish your body with essential nutrients. Packed with vitamins and minerals, they help in muscle recovery and boost overall performance. Drinking fresh juice post-workout ensures that your body gets the nutrients it needs to recover and build muscle efficiently

Fish

Fish is an exceptional source of high-quality protein and omega-3 fatty acids, crucial for muscle growth and recovery. The protein supports muscle repair, while omega-3s reduce inflammation and improve joint health. Including fish in your diet ensures you’re providing your body with the nutrients it needs to build and maintain muscle effectively.
